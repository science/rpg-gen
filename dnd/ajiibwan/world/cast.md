# Cast of the Deserts of Ajiibwan

The people, allies, villains, and bystanders of the Ajiibwan campaign world — the prior party's companions, the powers they bargained with, and the foes they fought.

> Generated from the converted corpus (`assets/_raw/.extract/cat/people.md`, ~144 raw blocks from multiple readers). Duplicate captures of the same person have been merged; facts are unioned and source documents combined. Spellings are preserved as written in the sources (B'rath, Farrav'n, m'Thorsz, A''ki, etc.). Where two captures disagree, both facts are kept and the discrepancy flagged.

## Prior Player Characters

The standing roster of the previous campaign — the adventurers (and permanent NPC companions) who explored Ajiibwan. Day numbers are in-world campaign days.

**A''ki** (of the Whisper Warrior Clan) — Aarakocra (Hawk Owl) Monk, later Monk/Warlock; she/her. A sunspirit, day-born monk on her clan's *flyabout* coming-of-age journey, mastering monk skills toward ascension to the undying Whisper Warriors. Acrobatic mastery; can teach acrobatics. Core party member: rescue missions, sphinx excavations, demon battles, major cult operations. Converted to Tanil worship (Day 26). Trains monk apprentices Waru and Masym. Status: active.
`sources:` Journal; Basq - Kalinore's Aftermath.md

**Kai** (Kaikuva) — Leonin Barbarian (wild magic); she/her. A "big angry lion woman" of few words, seeking to restore her Pride (homelands) conquered by gnoll druids and a powerful witch. Wild magic backfires when she is angered (psychic AoE). Carries a white-metal (Ukarran) longsword and, by Day 21, the Ukkar White Death Hammer. Joined Day 13; central to sphinx ops, demon battles, slave rescue, cult operations. Converted to Tanil worship (Day 26). Status: active.
`sources:` Journal

**Silverin** — Half-Elf Druid (Circle of the Moon), later multiclass Warlock (by Day 91); they/them. Raven-haired, coppered skin, androgynous; territorial and arrogant. Wild-shapes into tigers, rats, and other animals; fluent in Bokkaran. Core member from early campaign; key in sphinx ops, demon battles, cult assaults; develops ties with dryads and Tanil (converted Day 26). Status: active.
`sources:` Journal

**Thieri** — Human Rogue (Inquisitive), later multiclass Artificer (by Day 91); he/him; lv6+. Middle-aged, balding under a dramatic hat, foppish, fond of drink, fine red cloak. Skilled blade; gained Bokkaran fluency after touching a red sphere in a temple (Day 13); develops alchemy/infusions. Core member; runs interrogations; made tailor connections in Tel A'bib. Family friend / close relation of foreign prince Malik Qumar. Status: active.
`sources:` Journal; The Dark Arts of Diplomacy: The Holy Cities.md

**Jiyloo** — Human Barbarian (Desert Barbarian); desert-born. Former metal-working apprentice to merchant Feltan; follower of Doorne (Dornian practices); frenzies in combat; owns a camel; carries a great axe. 39+ games with the party; equal loot share from Day 24; rescued enslaved people (Day 25); briefly returned to Tel A'bib by Day 83. Status: active core companion.
*Note:* The world-lore cluster also records a level-1 "Jiyloo Ubbett," barbarian assistant to Feltan — almost certainly this same character before joining the party. A separate Saragubi capture describes a "Jiyloo, horseman" aiding against a sandworm en route to Gnoll village Gila (traveling with monk apprentices Waru and Masym, trained by A''ki); and a B'rath capture describes "Jiyloo, female Tel A'bib merchant/guide." These later captures conflict on gender/role and may be different people sharing the name, or loose retellings of the companion — kept separate below where roles diverge.
`sources:` Journal; Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands; Basq - Kalinore's Aftermath.md; Return to Mesos.md

**Mica** — Earth Genasi Ranger; she/her. Quartz skin glows in morning light; stoic; eager for desert secrets; casts Pass Without a Trace. Joined Day 11; Tomb of Ancestors, sphinx/cult assault, shrine investigations. Status: active.
`sources:` Journal

**Millis** — Human Paladin (Oath of Redemption); she/her, 20s, shaved head, chain shirt. Follower of Ilmater (the Broken God); polearm master; officiates weddings; the party's moral voice (chose to incapacitate rather than kill, Day 9). Early caravan investigation (Day 3-4), sphinx, demon battles. Status: active but less present later.
`sources:` Journal

**Mort** — Tortle Druid (Circle of Spores); he/him; lv2. Practical and gentle; uses Shillelagh with spore effects; speaks kindly with the skunk Agnes. Joined Day 9; cult assault at Oasis of Sia, sphinx, demon battles. Status: active.
`sources:` Journal

**Yasch** — Kobold Ranger; she/her; lv4. Seeking fresh organs for a client; makes pickled eggs; fast friends with Gus over a love of "shinies"; aware of elementals, fiends, undead. Zebra-people encounter (Day 2), undead-knight grave robbing (Day 8), sphinx, demon battles, cult assault. Status: active (reduced later).
*Note:* A bare "Yasch — contact" appears in the Mesos' Dock pre-adventure network list; likely the same name referenced, no detail.
`sources:` Journal; Mesos' Dock.md

**Quin** (Harlequin) — Wood Elf Ranger; he/him; lv2. Suggests travel routes; initially suspicious, then welcomed. Joined Day 10; boar battle and journey to Durgan's Rest; clashed with Grey Dust over tactics. Status: active, then vanished from later records.
`sources:` Journal

**Maeve** — Half-Orc Bard. Storyteller/chronicler who flatters adventurers and records their deeds; capable healer (Aid); wields Dissonant Whispers and Vicious Mockery. Joined Day 40; Black Water Cult fortress assault (Day 40-42), Sea of Souls monster fights (Day 43), Al Faiz expedition. Status: active.
`sources:` Journal

**Sylsira** — Halfling Rogue; she/her. Very acrobatic; lockpicker and ruin investigator; seeking artifacts of her own; meticulous documenter. Joined by Day 27; Saragubi ops, Black Water fortress (Day 40-42), Sea of Souls crypt, Bokkaran digs. Friend of the antiquities buff Duli. Status: active.
`sources:` Journal; Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands

**Alel** — Human Cleric; older, white-haired; mace and crossbow. A former Imperial high inquisitor now seeking redemption, looking for a source of magic to right a past wrong and return home. Joined Day 23; sphinx exploration, floating-orb demon battles, holy-city investigations. Status: active.
`sources:` Journal

**Arry** (The Arrow) — Human Archer; ~18, gender-ambiguous, red-haired. New to the area; searching for missing parents; friendly but odd; skilled with the bow. Joined Day 23; demon battles, sphinx, gnoll encounters, holy-city ops; pursued Black Water Cult leads. Status: active.
`sources:` Journal

**Graeae** — Half-Elf Rogue; she/her; 102 years old. Retired bounty hunter; money-oriented; secretive; rapier, bow, stealth, card magic. Joined Day 23; floating-orb demon battles, sphinx investigations. Status: active.
`sources:` Journal

**Rhys** — Dwarf Fighter; he/him; lv2. Seeking riches to restore his home to glory. Joined Day 23; demon battles (knocked unconscious at the floating orb), sphinx exploration. Status: active.
`sources:` Journal

**Serinde** — Elf, from the village of Dimark (northwest of Tel A'bib). Rescued by the party from a slave caravan (Day 25). From the Black Smoke area, whose village uses a Bokkaran spell engine and a totem warding off Bokkaran curses; revealed the engine's location. Status: alive, indebted ally.
`sources:` Journal

**Dain Ironheart** — Dwarf; B'rath region. Traveled with the party toward B'rath, then departed on receiving a message to handle business with his friend Tsendur; absent from the B'rath/Mesos operations. Status: active but split off.

**Rienas** — half-caste adventurer; Kalas → Hothme. Delivered a package from a Vanic official in Kalas to Clan Gideon, who then recruited her into the party for the Hothme residence period; described as a typical "desert-addicted" adventurer. Status: active party associate.

**Drupah** — party member seeking Ukar knowledge (referenced as a companion in A''ki, Sylsira, and Mesos entries; no standalone block in the corpus).
`sources:` Journal; Freeing Mesos.md

## Allies & Recurring NPCs

**Elmingwed** (The Merchant Lord) — Elf merchant of Tel A'bib (northern section; mansion with a library tower). Wealthy patron, member of the Athyes religion, expert in ancient artifacts and Desert Script (Bokkaran); knows scholars at Bridge City and the orc at Durgan's Rest. Identified artifacts for the party and supplied soldiers/equipment for the Black Water Cult assault at Oasis of Sia in exchange for 40% of treasure and first dibs. Key information broker. Employs the soldier Abet Phera; allied with Elruen (elf magician) in Bridge(d) City. Status: alive, established patron.
*Note:* Race recorded as "elf" in journal/lore but "race unknown" in one Mesos' Dock capture.
`sources:` Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands; Journal; Mesos' Dock.md

**Tsendur** — Orc (one capture says half-orc) scholar/magician of Ancient Bokkaran; lives in an impressive homestead at Durgan's Rest (Ordurt), a mountain town. Speaks Bokkaran; assistants Kada and Kayta (dwarves); corresponds with party members learning the language and with Bridge City scholars. Encountered Days 10-11 (eager to teach Bokkaran); attacked by Maghiel and dark elves (Day 14); identifies artifacts for white metal. Friend of Dain Ironheart. Status: alive, established ally and information source.
`sources:` Journal; Mesos' Dock.md

**Essen** — Human Rogue (aspiring Scout); Tel A'bib. Assistant to the traveling-gear merchant Al-Esellin. Brave fighter; frequent party guide. His younger brother Tane was kidnapped (went missing in the Eye of Kings caves during the Anauld's Eve incident) and later took part in a Tomb of Ancestors rescue. Permanent companion with equal loot claim; left in charge of Jiyloo's affairs during absences. Status: alive, trusted.
`sources:` Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands; Journal; Return to Mesos.md

**Vol** — Innkeeper of the New Moon tavern in Tel A'bib — the party's favorite inn; holds the party's strongbox. Status: alive, service provider.
`sources:` Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands; Journal

**Meso / Mesos** (Mesos Ukar; the Titan King) — Gargantuan celestial titan, neutral good, ~60 ft, NG (400 HP, AC 18). Speaks Common/Imperial, Giant, Primordial, Ukar; telepathy 120 ft; known for "Meso's Bane." Imprisoned for eons by a Bokkaran Dream Catcher in an underground mud-pool prison at B'rath; body dismembered and scattered; teaches B'rath children the Ukar language. Seeks the Khezurai ring and bodily reconstruction to escape and reach the Sefurah Gardens / Al-Faiz tree. The party recovered the titan's finger and parts, disabled the Dream Catcher, and partly freed/reconstructed him; he turns toward alliance and shares lore of the Khezurai rings, Al-Faiz, Sefurah Springs, and Bokkaran war history. Status: partially freed, semi-conscious, communicative.
*Note:* The world-lore cluster places him in the Saragubi Desert (linked to Dimark / Ukar Federation); the B'rath cluster places his prison at B'rath. Spelled "Meso" and "Mesos."
`sources:` Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands; Freeing Mesos.md; Return to Mesos.md; The Titan's Finger and the Fire Beetles Adventure.md

**Barthazai Gideon** (Barthazai) — Dwarf; leader of Clan Gideon (Blue Diamond / Gideon merchant house), Hothme. Invests in goldsmiths, gems, potions, firearms, and magic; involved in Gate commerce with Dran and Ars Vaskul. Provides the party Saragubi maps, a faerie-iron carving key, intel on the Hidden Crypt near the Pyramid of the Lost King, and political guidance; accompanies them to meet the m'Thorsz and advises diplomacy. Husband: Gifrendi. Status: active, key political ally.
*Note:* World-lore lists "Barthazai Gideon" as clan leader; the Saragubi cluster lists "Barthazai" as Clan Gideon's leader — treated as one person.
`sources:` Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands; Finding the Crypt of the Saragubi Desert.md; Kalinore's Tomb adventure.md; Basq - Kalinore's Aftermath.md

**Burga** — Elf male wizard-scholar at Valeska's Needle (Casting Guild / library), Hothme; member of the Scholar's Guild. Thoughtful, studious, timid; specialist in Bokkaran lore. Holds Kalinore's enchanted music box (activates only for a bearer of Afra's Spark) but cannot activate it himself. Commissions the party to investigate Kalinore's Tomb; discovers they carry Afra's Spark. Status: active, scholar quest-giver.
`sources:` Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands; Kalinore's Tomb adventure.md

**Irissee** (Irrissee) — Half-elf female; court wizard of Hothme and Bokkaran scholar; advisor on strategy and law (Hothme City Council). Also described as a fighting warlock at Valeska's Needle. Status: active.
`sources:` Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands

**Tal El-Mazari** (Tal) — Spy/diplomat of the Holy Cities; the m'Thorsz's contact. Greets the party at the Sem La Vah dock; provides letters of introduction, district tours, lodging at Khrumai's Rest. Status: trusted informant, cooperative.
`sources:` The Dark Arts of Diplomacy: The Holy Cities.md

**Na'ilah Kopak** (Na'ilah) — Female desert scout commander, Holy Cities militia. Honor- and duty-driven but pragmatic; aids caravan defense and the Cult fort raid; central to negotiating the captured fort's future control. Rival of Gala dak. Status: ally.
`sources:` The Dark Arts: Caravan Rescue.md; The Dark Arts: Raid the Cult's Desert Fort.md

**Dr. Isilla Rissan** (Isilla) — Female human doctor and archaeologist, Holy Cities (Inferna Wadi). Expert on Ukar and Bokkaran civilizations; her gallery holds an active Ukar portal usable to infiltrate Cult HQ. Status: potential ally.
`sources:` The Dark Arts of Diplomacy: The Holy Cities.md; The Dark Arts: Raid the Cult's Desert Fort.md

**Mirza Hadrian Andruli** (Hadrian; Count/Lord Hadrian) — Male human military noble, Holy Cities (Zakutar); distant cousin of the Vanic Emperor. Commands loyal guard forces; seeks to prove the Cult threatens the Empire; can mobilize troops. Status: potential ally.
`sources:` The Dark Arts of Diplomacy: The Holy Cities.md; The Dark Arts: Raid the Cult's Desert Fort.md

**Tenetu** — Tugari female warrior-leader, Saragubi Desert; rides a white horse. Leads her tribe with scout captain Weshkhasi; knows of Charnel Keep but avoids it. Engages the party in ritual combat (first to 15 damage wins) and insists they take a minder (Onsun). Becomes a contact for desert access and trade routes. Status: active tribal leader.
`sources:` Basq - Kalinore's Aftermath.md; Kalinore's Chaos in the Saragubi Desert.md

**Weshkhasi** — Tugari female scout captain under Tenetu; skilled in desert navigation and creature avoidance. Status: active.
`sources:` Basq - Kalinore's Aftermath.md; Kalinore's Chaos in the Saragubi Desert.md

**Onsun** — Tugari female fighter and orphan (one name, mysterious origins); joined the tribe young. Assigned as the party's minder; travels with them to Charnel Keep and into the magical disturbances. Status: active party associate.
`sources:` Kalinore's Chaos in the Saragubi Desert.md

**Roshona Gideon** — Female dwarf, leader of Clan Gideon in the Holy Cities (Sheloah district); jeweled, braided beard. Welcomes the party at the private trading house; coordinates with loremaster Barazen and deep-desert trader Tormundgrin; briefs them on Holy Cities factions and trade.
`sources:` The Dark Arts of Diplomacy: The Holy Cities.md

**Vesagol** — Demon (fiend-cleric type), imprisoned in a glowing red pentacle in Charnel Keep's undead cavern, in constant pain as weak undead spawn around him. Can be freed, killed, or recruited as a temporary ally; knows the forbidden book that unlocks the Spectral Knights area and that the Knights must be killed; can help cast an escape spell using Shrine/Laboratory resources. Status: imprisoned (potentially liberated).
`sources:` Kalinore's Chaos in the Saragubi Desert.md

**Imroth** — Ancient Bokkaran god/spirit of performance (Bokkaran pantheon); maintains an altar in the Crypt of the Saragubi Desert. Challenges visitors to a performance competition; grants the "Second Sight" (True Seeing) boon and fine Bokkaran instruments to those who succeed. Status: active deity/spirit.
`sources:` Escaping the Crypt of the Saragubi Desert.md

**Ilymna** (Ilimyna) — Bokkaran warrior-general and Kalinore's true love; master of performance and cultural magic. Built a palace in Kalinore's Domain; her ghost resides there, guarded by spirits, and holds the second enchanted music box paired with Kalinore's. Provides Kalinore's history; full interaction requires synchronizing the music boxes. Status: deceased (ghost), bound to the palace.
`sources:` Kalinore's Tomb adventure.md

**Baz** — Cleric (race unknown), Holy Cities poisoned-well crisis. Helps the party treat the sick and dying; gains a ball invitation through their actions; becomes an intelligence contact. Status: ally.
`sources:` The Dark Arts of Diplomacy: The Holy Cities.md

**Rochig** — Male dwarf, imprisoned at the Cult desert fort and forced to tend its explosives cache. Skilled with explosives; if rescued, helps the party blow the fort walls. Status: prisoner who aids rescuers.
`sources:` The Dark Arts: Raid the Cult's Desert Fort.md

## Villains & Antagonists

**Maghiel** (variants: Magdaliel; "Maghiel Erdot"; "Bringer of Life"; "MG"; "MG") — Drow (Dark Elf) Wizard, the campaign's recurring arch-antagonist. Hollow, disfigured appearance; plated chest armor; highly skilled combatant. Hunts Bokkaran artifacts (especially depleted "wands") to open a magical box holding an object of great power. Trains Black Water Cultists in ritual magic; works to raise undead; manipulates/coordinates with the lich Kalinore at Charnel Keep; operates a ship on the Sea of Souls; sends taunting, library-seal-watermarked letters. Directs Cult excavation under the desert fort to reach the Sea of Souls and runs the well-poisoning campaigns. Intercepted the party's correspondence with Tsendur and attacked his home with fire-beings and dark-elf warriors (Day 14); reappeared as an apparition in the Bridge City College tower (Day 15, disintegrated when struck by a twilight scimitar); attacked at Hothme's Sacred Pool (fireballs, civilian deaths) while negotiating with Adric Cascabel; confronted on her Sea of Souls boat; linked to the Nalfeshnee summoning. Corresponds with Cardinal Sponfz. Status: active, extremely dangerous.
*Note:* Captured under several spellings/aliases across clusters (Maghiel, Magdaliel, "MG"). The Holy Cities "Maghiel" who corresponds with Cardinal Sponfz to open the magical box is the same figure; treated as one antagonist throughout.
`sources:` Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands; Lore - Primary Source materials; Journal; Kalinore's Tomb adventure.md; Basq - Kalinore's Aftermath.md; Kalinore's Chaos in the Saragubi Desert.md; Crisis at Kantaron Summit.md; Pursuing Nalfeshnee at the Kantaron Summit.md; The Dark Arts: Raid the Cult's Desert Fort.md; Freeing Mesos.md; Return to Mesos.md

**Kalinore** — Bokkaran wizard-king of ages past, now a lich. Built a palace-tomb complex for himself and his love Ilymna in the Saragubi Desert; bound to a phylactery coffin. Dangerous spellcaster (Paralyzing Touch, Ray of Frost, Magic Missile, Thunderwave, Fireball, Disrupt Life); resurrects undead minions; inflicts despair. Sealed in his tomb, fought by the party, escapes via magical bonfire near death; more fully released during the Charnel Keep arc, where he generates magical storms and spawns undead while allied with / manipulated by Maghiel. Status: undead (lich); active threat.
`sources:` Kalinore's Tomb adventure.md; Basq - Kalinore's Aftermath.md; Kalinore's Chaos in the Saragubi Desert.md

**Nalfeshnee** (initially the construct identity "Lados") — Balor demon, major antagonist of the Kantaron arc. Originally meant to be summoned/controlled by Duroigh through unwitting manipulation. Bite 5d10+5, claws 3d6+5, horrifying presence (DC15 WIS or frightened); spawns lesser demons; causes fires and magical disturbances. Grows in power as Duroigh works; escapes/fought at Kantaron Summit; final confrontation in the Sea of Souls crypt within an ancient Bokkaran facility, where the party must beat it and restore magical containment. Status: active major threat.
`sources:` Tinkerer's Workshop: Kantaroz Summit.md; Crisis at Kantaron Summit.md; Pursuing Nalfeshnee at the Kantaron Summit.md

**Duroigh** (Count Duroigh) — Magical tinkerer/wizard, Kantaron Summit (Tinkerer's Workshop beneath an ancient Bokkaran tower). Controls animated constructs powered by crystal "brains" that imprison enslaved creature-souls; can control but not create/repair them. Unknowingly manipulated by Nalfeshnee into assembling a vessel for the demon's summoning. Holds Bokkaran spell scrolls (Mending, Encode Thoughts, Alarm, Catapult, Find Familiar, Blur). Status: active, possibly enslaved to the demon.
*Note:* Two captures — "Duroigh" (journal, inferred from Elmingwed Day 5 as the creator/commander of the crystal constructs that target Bokkaran artifacts; not yet confronted there) and "Count Duroigh"/"Duroigh" (Saragubi, confronted in the Tinkerer's Workshop). Same person; the journal frames him as an unconfronted threat behind the constructs, the Kantaron docs as the confronted antagonist.
`sources:` Journal; Tinkerer's Workshop: Kantaroz Summit.md; Crisis at Kantaron Summit.md

**Donol** (the Bokkaran Undead King) — Ancient Bokkaran warrior-king (mummy/lich) entombed in the Crypt of the Saragubi Desert; tomb of white metal inlaid with gold, door carvings of his conquests, guarded by four skeletal knights. Awakens when his sarcophagus is disturbed; defeating him collapses the tomb. Status: destroyed by the party.
`sources:` Finding the Crypt of the Saragubi Desert.md; Escaping the Crypt of the Saragubi Desert.md

**Gala dak** — Powerful merchant captain of Tel A'bib who built a secret slave-and-contraband network; holds leverage over Governor Kilmah Fari. The party captured and took over his compound, but his allies remain a threat (and he is a rival of the Holy Cities scout Na'ilah Kopak). Status: at-large.
`sources:` Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands

**Gnoll Druids** — Druids of gnoll origin who attacked Kai's Pride, enslaved her people, and burned their herds (wildfire magic); the existential threat behind Kai's backstory. The party encountered a gnoll in a caravan (Day 25, Kai reacted violently) and was warned away from Favarr'n (the gnoll city); the party avoids them and allies instead with other gnoll groups (Day 47, Saragubi). Status: historical enemies, avoided.
`sources:` Journal

## Factional & Regional Figures

### Hothme (Granite Empire)

**m'Thorsz / Dhallak Carn** — Dwarf ruler of Hothme. *Dhallak Carn* is the personal name; *m'Thorsz* is the ruling title. Restored order after a palace revolt ~20 years ago (ambiguous succession). Vain, ostentatious, abrupt, regal; came to power late in life; concerned with tax revenue and trade. Demands a share of the party's loot and compensation for the damage from Maghiel's Grand Forum attack; commissions them to investigate Saragubi issues and arrange Tugari trade routes; can grant residence/permanent status in Hothme. Royal secretary: Ilcond. Status: active, powerful patron/antagonist.
`sources:` Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands; Kalinore's Tomb adventure.md; Basq - Kalinore's Aftermath.md; Kalinore's Chaos in the Saragubi Desert.md

**Ilcond** — Dwarf bureaucrat; royal secretary to the m'Thorsz, Hothme. Conducts the party's initial interrogation, briefs etiquette/protocol, and negotiates loot shares on the m'Thorsz's behalf. Status: active functionary.
`sources:` Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands; Kalinore's Tomb adventure.md

**Adric Cascabel** — Ruthless patriarch / head of the Cascabel "Golden Camel" trading house, Hothme's richest merchant house; for two centuries their caravans crossed the Red Plains trading morenassa incense for gold, spices, gems, silks, and teas. Observed negotiating with Maghiel at the Sacred Pool during her attack. Status: active.
`sources:` Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands; Kalinore's Tomb adventure.md

**Gazrak** (Chancellor) — Dwarf chancellor of Hothme, allied with Dhallak Carn; manages taxes (Hothme City Council). Status: active.

**Bluzco** (City metrarch) — Powerful human desert merchant; Hothme's city metrarch, managing trade (Hothme City Council). Status: active.

**Gifrendi** — Husband of Barthazai Gideon (Gideon merchant house). Status: active.

**Duli** — History and Bokkaran-antiquities buff; great friend of Sylsira. Status: active.

**Ritabu Loklir** — Dwarf household manager hired by Clan Gideon to run the party's Hothme residence (furnishings, guards, staff). Status: active functionary.
`sources:` Kalinore's Chaos in the Saragubi Desert.md

**Gamul** — Dwarf, the m'Thorsz's official minder sent with the party to oversee a supply caravan into the Saragubi Desert; provides route guidance but is secretly trading weapons with the Tugari (his own agenda). Status: active, dual-purpose / possibly duplicitous.
*Note:* A bare "Gamul of m'Thorsz — court official" appears in the Mesos' Dock network list; same person.
`sources:` Basq - Kalinore's Aftermath.md; Mesos' Dock.md

**Dolasea** — Elf male wizard; arrogant, proud, difficult; leader of the Casting Guild at Valeska's Needle (Scholar's Guild). Present at the Sacred Pool when Maghiel attacks; associated with Burga's scholarly circle. Status: active.
`sources:` Lore - Ajiibwan Wilderlands; Kalinore's Tomb adventure.md

### Holy Cities (politics, factions, and notables)

**Begum Fatima Zaman** (Fatima) — Female human royalty governing the Ell Bastis district; pious patron of arts, temples, and religious institutions; publicly neutral but possibly a covert anti-Cult ally. Status: possible covert ally.

**Youssef al-Salim** — Male human merchant prince, Birjari; vast trade networks and economic influence; seeks trade stability; can mobilize troops against the Cult. Status: merchant ally.

**Amid Pharoun** — Male human merchant, Nabi Nageab; controls eastern trade; knows the Cult's desert-base location and sabotage plans. Status: merchant ally.
